About Viego

Connecting travelers with unforgettable experiences across Vietnam and Southeast Asia since 2025.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Viego is a project created by a group of six students from FPT University who share a common passion: showcasing the beauty of Vietnam’s tourism to the world.

Driven by our love for our homeland and a desire to promote its culture and landscapes, we built this platform to introduce stunning destinations, authentic experiences, and the unique values of Vietnam.

Though we are still students, we are committed to producing genuine, creative, and inspiring content in the hope of connecting both local and international travelers with the charm of Vietnam.

Our Mission & Values

Authentic Experiences

We believe in travel that goes beyond tourist attractions, connecting travelers with local cultures, traditions, and people.

Responsible Tourism

We’re committed to sustainable travel practices that respect local communities and preserve natural environments for future generations.

Exceptional Service

We strive to exceed expectations at every touchpoint, providing personalized attention and support throughout your journey.
